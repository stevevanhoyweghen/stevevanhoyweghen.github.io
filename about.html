About ...

I am a photographer and artist with a wide range of photographic and artistic interests.

Conceptual, surreal, generative, still-life, travel, urban and architectural themes are my favorite photography topics.

My technology based artwork is more recent. Technology is a natural way to express my creativity due to my backgroundas an information technology and electronics engineer with an appreciation for modern art, with a soft spot for kinetic installations. This, combined with my practical hands-on attitude, gives me the tools, skills and insights to create kinetic installations.
